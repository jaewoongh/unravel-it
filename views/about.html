WHAT IS UNRAVEL-IT

  • Unravel-it is a timeline based wiki.

    Think of Wikipedia but everything in a timeline format rather than resembling encyclopedia.

  • Unravel-it gives nice big pictures about complex subject.

    Sometimes getting information from news media or any other sources is like getting a tangled yarn. You can see some part of it, most likely the latest news, but it is not always easy to see it as a whole; how it began and how it came to light. Thanks to timeline format, you can easily unravel the subjects' current state, evolution to its beginning.

  • Unravel-it makes it easy to compare related topics.

    You can place two or more timelines onto one page with the same timescale. This enables you to clearly see what happened first and what next, and it might reveal a previously unseen relationship between topics.

  • Unravel-it is a collaborative editing system.

    You are free to edit timeline or even start your own one about subject you are interested in. Just make sure to give a good note for editing, and a solid reference link for new information.

TIMELINE VIEW

❶ Each entry in the wiki is an independent timeline.
It has title, summary, and a number of "tidbits."

❷ Tidbits are what makes up the timeline.
They are small bits of information about what happened when.

❸ Check icons.
You can make a change, see revision notes, and do more.

❹ Use the sidebar to get an ideal view.
Remember, Unravel-it is all about getting a nice big picture of complex subject.
